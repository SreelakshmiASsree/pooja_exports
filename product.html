Coconut Shell Charcol

The coconut shell has a high calorific value and can be used to produce steam, energy-rich gases, bio-oil, biochar etc. Coconut shell is a solid fuel and it is more suitable for pyrolysis process as it contains lower ash content, high volatile matter content and is available consistently. Coir

It is known for its low decomposition rate. We provide coir fibre that is free from pith (2-3%), and roots. Natural coir fibre is baled with the assistance of rust free iron hoops or plastic straps, to ensure its high quality and utility. Our products are available at competitive rates and we can customise the product as per client requirement. Coir fibre is used in the bedding industry, for automobile upholstery and for erosion control.
